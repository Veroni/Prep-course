Syrniky

Syrniky is a traditional Siberian dish which is usually served for breakfast. It is a variation of pancakes made with cottage cheese. It is very delisious and healthy, because it contains high percentage of proteine. Your best breakfast starts here!

Ingredients:
  • 200g cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1tbsp white unbleached flour
  • 1tbsp semolina
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 1tsp vanila sugar

Step1

Take all your ingredients and mix them in a large bowl.

Step2

Make slightly flattened round pieces of dough.

Step3

Place your pieces with a little bit of oil to the frying pan and cook over a medium heat for 5-7 min.

Step4

Enjoy your syrniky!
